# Supplementary Figure 12

## Overview

This figure evaluates senescence-associated activity, senescence-related transcriptional programs, and inflammatory signaling across pediatric lung developmental stages and PT-BPD samples using complementary molecular assays.

---

## 12A – Senescence-Associated β-Galactosidase Activity

Senescence-associated β-galactosidase (SA-β-Gal) activity was quantified in lung protein lysates using the Enzo Cellular Senescence Assay Kit according to the manufacturer's instructions.

Fluorescence measurements were normalized to total protein concentration and compared across developmental groups and PT-BPD samples.

---

## 12B – NanoString nCounter Senescence Gene Expression

Expression of senescence-associated genes was quantified using the NanoString nCounter platform.

Normalized transcript counts were used to generate a heatmap displaying relative gene expression across developmental stages and PT-BPD samples. Values were transformed to row Z-scores for visualization.

---

## 12C – Luminex Multiplex Cytokine Analysis

Protein concentrations of inflammatory cytokines associated with senescence and tissue remodeling were quantified using Luminex multiplex immunoassays.

Analytes shown include IL-6, MIP-1β, IL-1Rα, TNF-α, VEGF, and G-CSF. Protein concentrations were normalized to total protein input and compared across developmental groups and PT-BPD samples.

---

## 12D – Luminex Growth Factor and SASP Profiling

Additional senescence-associated secretory phenotype (SASP) mediators and growth factors were quantified using Luminex multiplex immunoassays.

Protein concentrations were normalized to total protein input and compared across developmental groups and PT-BPD samples.
