# Supplementary Figure 13

## Overview

This figure evaluates senescence-associated secretory phenotype (SASP) factors and cellular senescence in irradiated pediatric lung epithelial cells following p21 inhibitor treatment.

---

## 13A – Luminex Multiplex Protein Analysis

Protein concentrations of SASP-associated cytokines, chemokines, and growth factors were quantified using Luminex multiplex immunoassays.

Analytes shown include IL-6, PAI-1 (SERPINE1), MIP-1β, G-CSF, RANTES (CCL5), VEGF, FGF-basic, and IL-8.

Protein concentrations were normalized to total protein input and compared across Control, Irradiation (IR), IR + p21 Inhibitor, and p21 Inhibitor treatment groups.

---

## 13B – Senescence-Associated β-Galactosidase Staining

Cellular senescence was evaluated using the Abcam Senescence Detection Kit according to the manufacturer's instructions.

Representative brightfield images and corresponding SA-β-Gal staining images are shown for Control, Irradiation (IR), IR + p21 Inhibitor, and p21 Inhibitor treatment groups.

Blue-green SA-β-Gal-positive cells indicate senescence-associated β-galactosidase activity.
